# Digital Wellbeing Courses

A collection of self-paced courses for digital wellbeing: parenting around screens, reclaiming your own attention, and supporting a loved one with screen overuse. Each course includes modules, lessons, and practical challenges.

## Courses

| Course | Description | Audience | Duration | Folder |
|--------|-------------|----------|----------|--------|
| **Digital Parenting Foundations** | Helping Your Child Build Healthy Screen Habits | Parents | ~3 hours | [01_digital_parenting_foundations](courses/01_digital_parenting_foundations/) |
| **Reclaim Your Attention** | Healing Your Relationship With Screens | Self | ~3 hours | [02_reclaim_your_attention](courses/02_reclaim_your_attention/) |
| **Supporting Your Loved Ones** | Care, Boundaries, and Better Conversations | Partners, parents, friends of someone with heavy screen use | ~2.5 hours | [03_supporting_your_loved_ones](courses/03_supporting_your_loved_ones/) |
| **Supporting a Friend** | Empathy, Boundaries, and Small Steps | Friends of someone with heavy screen use | ~2.5 hours | [04_supporting_a_friend](courses/04_supporting_a_friend/) |

Each course folder contains:

- **`*.yml`** — Course metadata (title, goals, modules, lessons, gamification).
- **`*.md`** — Course content in Markdown.
